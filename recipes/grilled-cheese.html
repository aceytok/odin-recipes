Grilled Cheese

Description

A recipe for making a delicious grilled cheese sandwich from Allrecipes

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 3 tablespoons of butter, divided
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Butter the bread on one side and place one slice with the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet
  2. Top with cheese, then place the other slice of bread on top(butter-side up)
  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip
  4. Continue cooking and flipping regularly until cheese is melted
