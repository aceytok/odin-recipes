World's Best Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for the World's Best Lasagna that was created by John Chandler and submitted to Allrecipes

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna accordingly; meat sauce -> noodles -> ricotta mixture -> mozzarella slices -> meat sauce -> parmesan cheese -> repeat, then top with remaining parmesan cheese
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
