Minute Breakfast Burrito

Description

A fast DIY breakfast that's made in minutes with scrambled eggs, salsa, and cheese all wrapped up inside a tortilla from Allrecipes

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons salsa
  • 1 slice American cheese
  • 1 tortilla

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Spray a bowl with non-stick cooking spray. Crack eggs into bowl, add the salsa, and mix together
  3. Microwave on high for 1 minute, stir, and cook for another minute or until mixture firms up
  4. Place cheese in center of tortilla and top with egg mixture
  5. Wrap it all up like a burrito
